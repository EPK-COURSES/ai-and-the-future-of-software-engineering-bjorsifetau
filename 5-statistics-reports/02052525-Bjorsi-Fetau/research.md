## Trustworthy AI and Industry 5.0
According to Vyhmeister and Castane (2026), trustworthy AI is becoming an important part of Industry 5.0 and modern industrial systems. The study reviews how artificial intelligence is being used in industry while emphasizing the importance of ethics, transparency, security, and human-centered design.
The research explains that Industry 5.0 focuses on collaboration between humans and intelligent technologies rather than replacing people completely. AI systems can improve productivity, support decision-making, and optimize industrial processes while keeping human values at the center of development.
The study also highlights several challenges related to AI adoption, including privacy concerns, bias in AI systems, lack of transparency, and security risks. Organizations must ensure that AI technologies are reliable, fair, and accountable before implementing them in critical environments.
Another important finding is that successful AI adoption requires trust between humans and technology. Companies must establish ethical guidelines and governance practices to ensure responsible use of AI systems.
Overall, the research shows that trustworthy AI can provide significant benefits for Industry 5.0 while maintaining ethical standards and supporting human-centered innovation.
