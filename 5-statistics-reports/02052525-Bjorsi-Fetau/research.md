## Trustworthy AI and Industry 5.0
According to Vyhmeister and Castane (2026), trustworthy AI is becoming an important part of Industry 5.0 and modern industrial systems. The study reviews how artificial intelligence is being used in industry while emphasizing the importance of ethics, transparency, security, and human-centered design.
The research explains that Industry 5.0 focuses on collaboration between humans and intelligent technologies rather than replacing people completely. AI systems can improve productivity, support decision-making, and optimize industrial processes while keeping human values at the center of development.
The study also highlights several challenges related to AI adoption, including privacy concerns, bias in AI systems, lack of transparency, and security risks. Organizations must ensure that AI technologies are reliable, fair, and accountable before implementing them in critical environments.
Another important finding is that successful AI adoption requires trust between humans and technology. Companies must establish ethical guidelines and governance practices to ensure responsible use of AI systems.
Overall, the research shows that trustworthy AI can provide significant benefits for Industry 5.0 while maintaining ethical standards and supporting human-centered innovation.

## Industrial Artificial Intelligence and Industry 4.0
According to Lee et al. (2018), Industrial Artificial Intelligence (Industrial AI) is becoming a key technology in Industry 4.0 manufacturing systems. The authors explain that traditional manufacturing is changing because companies are increasingly using artificial intelligence, big data analytics, cloud computing, and cyber-physical systems to improve industrial performance and productivity. These technologies allow organizations to collect, process, and analyze large amounts of data in real time.
The study describes Industrial AI as a systematic approach that connects academic AI research with practical industrial applications. Instead of relying only on human experience, Industrial AI uses data-driven methods to support decision-making, monitor equipment performance, and improve manufacturing processes. This approach helps companies identify problems earlier and make more informed decisions.
One of the main benefits discussed in the research is predictive maintenance. AI systems can analyze machine data and detect patterns that indicate potential equipment failures before they occur. As a result, companies can reduce downtime, lower maintenance costs, and increase operational efficiency. Industrial AI can also improve product quality by continuously monitoring manufacturing processes and identifying abnormalities.
The authors highlight several important technologies that support Industrial AI. These include analytics technology, big data technology, cloud platforms, domain knowledge, and evidence-based validation. Together, these elements create an ecosystem that allows organizations to transform raw data into useful information and practical business solutions.
The research also emphasizes the importance of smart manufacturing. Industry 4.0 systems can connect machines, sensors, and digital platforms, allowing information to be shared automatically across production environments. This connectivity improves communication, increases flexibility, and enables faster responses to changing market demands.
However, the study identifies several challenges associated with Industrial AI. One challenge is data quality because AI systems require accurate and reliable data to produce effective results. Poor-quality data can lead to incorrect predictions and poor decisions. Another challenge is cybersecurity, as connected manufacturing systems may become vulnerable to cyberattacks. Machine-to-machine communication and system integration also require careful management to ensure reliability and security.
Overall, Lee et al. (2018) conclude that Industrial AI has the potential to transform modern manufacturing by improving productivity, efficiency, and decision-making. The research demonstrates that successful implementation of AI technologies can help industries become more intelligent, resilient, and competitive while supporting the goals of Industry 4.0.

## Trustworthy Industrial AI Applications
According to Leutner et al. (2026), artificial intelligence has become an important technology in Industry 4.0 because it helps companies improve productivity, flexibility, and efficiency. Industrial AI is used in areas such as process optimization, quality control, and predictive maintenance. By analyzing large amounts of production data, AI systems can help organizations make better decisions and improve manufacturing performance.
The research explains that many companies still face difficulties when implementing AI technologies. One challenge is the quality of data because AI models require accurate and reliable information to produce useful results. Another challenge is the complexity of AI systems, which are often difficult to understand and explain. Companies also need employees with the necessary technical knowledge to manage AI applications effectively.
The authors propose a framework for developing trustworthy industrial AI applications. The framework is based on four main dimensions: transparency, robustness, safety, and adaptivity. Transparency helps users understand how AI systems make decisions. Robustness ensures that AI models produce reliable results. Safety focuses on preventing harmful behavior when AI encounters abnormal situations. Adaptivity allows AI systems to adjust to changes in their environment over time.
The study also emphasizes the importance of trust in AI. Manufacturing companies are more likely to adopt AI technologies when they are reliable, understandable, and secure. In addition, regulatory requirements such as the European AI Act are increasing the need for trustworthy and responsible AI systems.
Overall, the research shows that AI can provide significant benefits for industry, but organizations must focus on transparency, safety, and reliability to ensure successful implementation and long-term adoption.
